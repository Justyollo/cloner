Mobile Banking

Now that you’re all set with a bank account, you’ll want to use all the tools available to make sure you can track that hard-earned money. The easiest way for even the busiest person on the go is to download your Skye Trust’s app onto your smartphone—it’s free and you’ll have access to your banking information right at your fingertips. .

You can track your spending, pay bills, and make sure that any deposits you’re expecting went through. While you cannot make cash transactions on the app, you can deposit checks with your phone by using the camera. That’s a saved trip to the ATM!.

How Online Banking Works

Banking online means accessing your bank account and carrying out financial transactions through the internet on your smartphone, tablet or computer. It's quick, usually free and allows you to do tasks, such as paying bills and transferring money, without having to visit or call your bank.

  • Opening accounts
  • Transferring funds
  • Applying for loans
  • Depositing checks
  • Staying informed
  • 24/7 on time services
